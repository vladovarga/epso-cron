    Job opportunities

    Looking for a job with the EU institutions or agencies?

    Search our database of available job opportunities for permanent and temporary positions, including contract agent posts, traineeships and secondments. 

    Selection procedures for temporary staff are generally organised by the institutions and agencies themselves, rather than by EPSO. 

    Visit our dedicated page to find the job vacancies websites of the institutions. The content of the agencies’ websites and vacancy notices therein is not EPSO's responsibility.

    Please note that not all available vacancies are displayed on this site and some positions may only be open to successful candidates of Contract Agent Selection (CAST) procedures.
